Room Designer

Students should say, "This is my room. I have ..."
Objects are best described following this model:
"I have [number] [size] [color] [thing]."
Examples:
"I have ten big blue windows."
"I have a small green chair."
"I have many mini rainbow clocks."

Students can change the type of object by pressing ▲▼.
Students can cycle through the colors by pressing ◀▶.
Students can flip an object by pressing ⇄.
If a student holds down CLEAR until it is flashing, it will clear everything! Wait a bit and it will disarm.
If a student presses 📷, it will capture a .PNG image of the current room.
If a student presses 💾, the room arrangement will be saved, and we should be able to see it again!

児童は「This is my room. I have ...」と言います。
次のモデルにそって言います。
「I have [数] [大小] [色] [物].」
例：
"I have ten big blue windows."「私は大きい青い窓を10枚持っています。」
"I have a small green chair."「私は小さい緑のいすを1つ持っています。」
"I have many mini rainbow clocks."「私は小さい虹色の時計をたくさん持っています。」

児童は▲▼を押すと、物の種類を変えます。
◀▶を押すと、色を変えます。
⇄を押すと、物を反転します。
CLEARを光るまで長押しすると、全部消えます。少し待つと解除します。
📷を押すと、今の部屋を.PNG画像で保存します。
💾を押すと、部屋の配置を保存します。あとでまた見られます！
